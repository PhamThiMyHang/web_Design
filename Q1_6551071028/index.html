eCommerce

Ecommerce or electronic commerce, a subset of ebusiness, e-commerce

is the purchasing, and exchanging of goods and services over computer

networks (such as internet) through which transaction or terms of the sale

performed electronically.

eCommerce requirements
Shopping cart Merchant account
Processing gateway Digital Certificate

Ecommerce can be broken into four main categories

  • B2B (Business-to-Business)

  • B2C (Business-to-Consumer)

  • B2B (Consumer-to-Business)

  • B2B (Consumer-to-Consumer)
